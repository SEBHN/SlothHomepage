About our SlothPi

Deep in the northern jungles of brasil we found a very special little sloth. When we found him, he cuddled really hard with his pi formed bambus branch. It turned out, this was not just a coincidence. This sloth seems to have a strong preference for maths. The little guy showed us how to convert from Binary numbers to Hex and how to calculate out human ages to sloth ages. For this we named your small friend SlothPi and decided to share all his knoledge with the world.
